    • About Us

    Hi there, Southernites. We are a food delivery website which mainly operated for Southern University College and by Southern University College Students. The founders of the website are Tan Kayyi, Hun Zu Rong, Kuganraj and Ng Jia King. The service is founded on January 2022 with the collaboration of the founders.

    aim

    create job oppurtunities and service to everyone in Southern University College at a reasonable price

    functionality

    Provides the functionality for customers to place their order and supply necessary details

    audience

    Southern University College students who staying at the hostel meanwhile looking for food at affordable price

    Frequently Asked Questions

    SouthernFoodie is a one stop solution for mainly Southern University College students to buy food at affordable price. Basically, its your kitchen a few clicks away to dine your favourite food.

    At first, you login. Then type in the your current location in the search box (eg.JB). We will then display the available shops that can cater to your requests

    SouthernFoodie offers you 2 choices at the moment. First, credit card option where you have to enter your credit card information. Second, cash on delivery option where you have pay the rider on the successful delivery

    We promise to deliver our food within half an hour. With each delivery, we provide your phone number and location to the rider so that we can track your delivery in real time
